FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about working together

How much should I budget for ad spend?

+
For most Kenyan businesses, I recommend starting with KSh 50K-100K monthly ad spend minimum. This allows enough data to test and optimize. Once profitable, we scale up. The management fee is separate from ad spend.

How long before I see results?

+
You'll see traffic and engagement immediately. For e-commerce, first sales typically happen within 1-2 weeks. Full campaign optimization takes 30-60 days. Most clients achieve profitable ROAS within 90 days.

Do you work with businesses outside Nairobi?

+
Absolutely! I work with clients across Kenya - Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, and everywhere in between. Everything is done remotely via WhatsApp, Zoom, and email.

What industries do you specialize in?

+
I've successfully run campaigns for e-commerce, restaurants, real estate, education, beauty/cosmetics, fashion, fitness, and professional services. If you sell to consumers in Kenya, I can help you scale.

Do you create the ad content too?

+
Yes! All packages include ad creative design and copywriting. I create static images, video ads, carousel ads, and write all ad copy. You just provide product photos/info and brand guidelines.

What's your minimum commitment?

+
I recommend a minimum 3-month commitment to allow proper testing and optimization. However, contracts are month-to-month - you can pause or cancel anytime with 30 days notice if you're not seeing results.
